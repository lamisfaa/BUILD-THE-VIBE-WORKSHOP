# DAY 2 — Competition Day 🏗️  
## Build the Vibe Bootcamp

Welcome to Day 2 of **Build the Vibe**!

Today’s challenge focuses on:
- Prompt Engineering
- Creativity
- Product Thinking
- AI-assisted development
- Fast iteration

---

# ⏰ Competition Duration

Teams will have:
- **1 hour to 1.5 hours**

to design, build, refine, and discuss their project.

⚠️ Teams are NOT expected to perform advanced technical setup.

All work should remain within the workshop scope:
- Prompting
- Iteration
- Copy/paste workflow
- Running the project locally
- Deploying to Vercel

---

# 🎯 Part 1 — Core Scenario (Required)

Each team receives:
- An event/company scenario
- A required website or app concept

Example:
> “Create a festival management platform for a music festival.”

---

# ✅ Minimum Requirements

Every project should include:
- Landing page
- Clear branding/theme
- Schedule or information section
- Interactive cards/sections
- Registration button or call-to-action
- Responsive/mobile-friendly design

This ensures all teams build something comparable.

---

# ⭐ Part 2 — Expansion & Iteration Challenges

Teams are encouraged to continue improving and refining their project throughout the competition.

The goal is NOT to finish quickly — the goal is to:
- Iterate
- Refine prompts
- Improve the experience
- Expand the product creatively
- Polish the final result

---

# 💡 Suggested Expansion Features

These should all be achievable primarily through prompting and AI-generated code.

## 🎨 Branding & Visual Identity
Examples:
- Strong color palette
- Consistent visual theme
- Custom hero sections
- Animations/transitions
- Creative typography
- AI-generated branding/copy

---

## ⚡ Interactive Features
Examples:
- Countdown timer
- Search/filter system
- Interactive schedule
- Dark mode
- Hover animations
- Expandable cards
- Multi-section navigation

---

## 🧠 AI-Assisted Product Features
Examples:
- Personalized recommendations
- Smart schedule ideas
- AI-generated event descriptions
- Chatbot-style UI
- AI-generated FAQs
- AI-generated artist/speaker bios

⚠️ These features do NOT need real AI integrations or APIs.

Creativity and user experience matter more than technical complexity.

---

## 📱 UX & Polish Improvements
Examples:
- Better mobile responsiveness
- Improved layouts
- Accessibility improvements
- Cleaner navigation
- Better spacing/visual hierarchy
- Additional polished pages

---

# 🧠 Part 3 — Prompt Engineering Evaluation

Teams must demonstrate:
- Their original prompt
- At least 2 prompt iterations/refinements
- How they improved outputs over time
- How prompting affected the final product

The competition rewards:
- Smart prompting
- Iteration
- Refinement
- Creativity
- Product thinking

—not advanced engineering experience.

---

# 📝 Final Judging Rubric (100 Points)

## 🎨 Product Design & User Experience — 25 pts
Judge:
- Visual quality
- Branding consistency
- Responsiveness
- User experience
- Overall polish

---

## ⚡ Functionality & Features — 25 pts
Judge:
- Working sections/components
- Interactive elements
- Feature completeness
- Smooth experience
- Quality of implementation

---

## 💡 Creativity & Product Thinking — 20 pts
Judge:
- Originality
- Creative interpretation of scenario
- Interesting ideas/features
- Strong product vision

---

## 🧠 Prompt Engineering & Iteration — 20 pts
Judge:
- Prompt quality
- Prompt refinement
- Smart iteration process
- Effective use of AI tools
- Ability to improve outputs through prompting

---

## 🎤 Communication — 10 pts
Judge:(tech assistants will pass by each time and ask them to show their work)
- Demo clarity
- Team explanation
- Ability to explain workflow and decisions
- Communication skills

---

# 🌟 Bonus Points (Up to +20)

Optional improvements may earn bonus points.

Examples:
- Exceptional branding
- Advanced polish
- Creative UI interactions
- Additional pages/features
- Strong mobile experience
- Unique AI-assisted experiences
- Highly refined prompt workflow

---

# 🎤 Questions Judges May Ask

## Prompt Questions
- What was your starting prompt?
- How did your prompts evolve?
- What prompt gave the best result?
- What challenge did prompting help solve?

---

## Product Questions
- Why did you design it this way?
- What feature are you most proud of?
- What would you improve with more time?

---

## Workflow Questions
- What changed the most during iteration?
- Did AI generate anything unusable?
- How did you refine or improve it?

---

# 🚀 Competition Goal

The goal is not traditional coding.

The goal is to:
- Improve existing ideas
- Think creatively
- Iterate effectively
- Use AI tools intelligently
- Transform ideas into polished products using prompting and experimentation (**Vibe Coding**)
